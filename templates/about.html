About Us

Lily, Julia, and Jenny met at Google Computer Science Summer Institute (CSSI) in 2018. Since they were about to enter college and were focusing on finding the right roommate, they decided to use their new programming skills to create an app that matches college students to the right roommate for them, every time.

They hope you and future college students have ROFL (Roommates on Fantastic Levels) who always find time to ROFL (Roll on the Floor Laughing)!
